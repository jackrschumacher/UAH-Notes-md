# Being "in the flow"
- Oftentimes, I find that I am in the flow most when I am writing, both in the context of writing papers and other such written documents, and also in the way of writing code- I find that after some time, I can become intensely focused on the task - and what must be done in order for me to accomplish my objectives. This can especially happen when writing code- as this is something that I do more often than I write. When writing code- there is a programmatic approach to writing than with 'creative' writing- that is- there is an end goal- something that must be accomplished in order to call the project/writing a success.
