- **Domestic organizations** operate within a single country
- **International organizations** based primary within a single country but have continuing international transactions
- **Multinational corporation** a company that has significant operations in more than 1 country
- ![image.png](../assets/image_1769749920827_0.png){:height 526, :width 544}
- **Expatriate** - lives or works in a country where they do not have citizenship
- **Host country national** - An organization member who is a citizen of the country in which the facility of a foreign-based org is located
- **Third country national** - An organization member who is a citizen of one country and works in another country for an org headquartered in another country
- **Repatriation** - process of brining individuals who have been working abroad to their home country
- **Direct investing** -  uses the assets of one company to purchase the operating assets
- **International joint venture** - a partnership formed between a company in one country and a company in another country for the purpose of pursuing some mutually desirable business undertaking
- **International market agreement** - an arrangement among a cluster of countries that facilitates a high legal of trade among countries
- **Ethnocentric attitude** - belief that multinational corporations should regard home country practices as superior to others
- **Polycentric attitude** - reflects the belief that foreign managers know more about their area of control
- **Transnational organization** - global organization
