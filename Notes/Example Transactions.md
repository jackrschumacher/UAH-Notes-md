- # Examples for different accounts
- ## Products and Sales
- ### Sale Price transaction
	- For increasing sale price
		- Debit accounts receiveable
		- Credit sales revenue
- ### Product Cost transaction
	- Debit inventory
	- Credit cash
- ### Purchasing supplies
	- Credit supplies
	- Debit cash
- ## Paying salaries
	- Debit salaries account
	- Credit cash
